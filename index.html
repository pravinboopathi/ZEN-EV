What are benefits Of commercial EV charging stations?

The most common types of commercial EV charging stations are Level 2 chargers and DC fast chargers. Level 2 chargers use 240-volt AC power and can charge an electric car in a few hours, while DC fast chargers use direct current (DC) and can charge an electric car in as little as 30 minutes.

These chargers are located in public places such as parking lots, shopping malls, airports, gas stations, and along highways.
